# Breadfast — Spec Ad
## Campaign Objective

To increase sales by showing that Breadfast is not only for breakfast, but a fast and convenient option for different moments throughout the day.

## Insight

Many people associate Breadfast mainly with breakfast, while in reality, it offers much more than just breakfast.

At the same time, we all procrastinate and make excuses to put things off. Sometimes, one small excuse is enough to delay everything—like saying you cannot study because you have not had your coffee yet.
## Big Idea

No More Excuses.

## Concept

A sarcastic and fast-paced comedy ad about a high school student who keeps making excuses to avoid studying for her upcoming exams.

The ad starts with her mother telling her to study. The daughter immediately comes up with an excuse: she cannot study without coffee.

She orders from Breadfast, and the order arrives within seconds, highlighting the brand's speed. But the order is not just coffee—it has everything she needs.

With her main excuse gone, the mother keeps pushing her to study, while the daughter tries to come up with new excuses.

The ad will use fast cuts, sarcastic dialogue, and quick back-and-forth interactions inspired by the style of Ping Pong advertisements. The pace keeps getting faster as the excuses run out, creating a humorous and relatable situation.

The campaign starts with coffee as the first excuse, then expands to show that Breadfast offers much more than just breakfast.
