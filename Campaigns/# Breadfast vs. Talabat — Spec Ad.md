# Breadfast vs. Talabat — Spec Ad

## Campaign Objective

To create a humorous and memorable attack campaign that highlights Breadfast's fast delivery and reliable service through a playful comparison with Talabat.

## Insight

When people order food, they usually expect something to go wrong: an item might be missing, the food might arrive late, or the delivery person might have eaten something on the way.

## Big Idea

What if the order arrived so perfectly and quickly that the customer couldn't believe it?

## Concept

A sarcastic comedy ad built around the customer's disbelief that his Breadfast order arrived quickly and complete.

The humor comes from the customer expecting something to be missing, just like the common frustrations people associate with food delivery. However, everything is there—including the fries.

## Script

**Customer:** إنت جبت الفرايز بجد؟!

**Delivery Guy:** ءءء... آه يا فندم.

**Customer:** يعني معقول؟ لا، أكيد نسيت حاجة.

**Delivery Guy:** لا يا فندم، كل حاجة موجودة.

**Customer:** حتي الفرايز؟؟؟
**Delivery Guy:** لا إله إلا الله... حضرتك كويس يا فندم؟

The customer suddenly starts crying emotionally

He hugs the delivery guy and goes inside.

The delivery guy stands there confused, slaps his forehead, and says:

**Delivery Guy:** يا حول الله!

## Execution Style

- Sarcastic comedy
- Fast-paced dialogue
- Exaggerated reactions
- Relatable delivery frustrations
- Subtle competitor attack
- Unexpected ending
